Privacy Policy

Money Control  ·  Last updated: April 25, 2026

Money Control ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal finance application developed by Aman (developerlife69@gmail.com). This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is protected.

Data We Collect

  • Account information — email address used to sign in (Google, Apple, or email/password).
  • Financial transactions — amounts, categories, notes, and dates that you manually enter or import.
  • Budgets & recurring payments — budget limits and subscription schedules you configure.
  • Lent/borrowed money records — entries you create to track money lent to or borrowed from others.
  • Categories & rules — custom categories and SMS auto-categorisation rules you define.
  • App settings — theme preference, currency, onboarding state, stored locally via SharedPreferences.
  • SMS messages Pro feature — bank notification SMS on your device, accessed only with your explicit permission (see SMS section below).

SMS Data Collection & Use

READ_SMS permission is used exclusively to import bank transaction notifications.

When you grant the SMS permission, the app scans your inbox for messages from recognised bank sender IDs (e.g. "HDFCBK", "ICICIB"). It extracts only:

  • Transaction amount
  • Merchant or payee name
  • Transaction date and reference number

All SMS processing happens entirely on your device. No SMS body text, phone numbers, or contact names are transmitted to our servers or any third party. The extracted transaction fields are stored only in your personal Firestore document, which is protected by per-user security rules and is never shared.

You can revoke this permission at any time from Android Settings → Apps → Money Control → Permissions. Revoking the permission disables automatic SMS import but does not affect any data already imported.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To display your transaction history, budgets, and analytics inside the app.
  • To send you local notifications for recurring payments, subscription status changes, and budget alerts.
  • To generate PDF/CSV exports at your request.
  • To restore your data when you reinstall or sign in on a new device.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app features described above.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, protected by per-user security rules — each user can only read and write their own documents. Data at rest and in transit is encrypted by Firebase/Google infrastructure. We apply the principle of least privilege: no server-side code reads your data except during admin-triggered subscription approval (which accesses only subscription status fields).

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, crash reporting, and push notifications.
  • Google Play Billing — processes in-app subscription payments. We store only the purchase token and order ID returned by Google; your payment card details are never visible to us.
  • Google ML Kit — on-device OCR for receipt scanning. No image data leaves your device.

Each third-party service is governed by its own privacy policy. We do not sell your data to any party.

Biometric Authentication

If you enable app lock, biometric data (fingerprint or face) is processed entirely by your device's operating system via the Android BiometricPrompt API. We never access, store, or transmit biometric data.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Data Management → Delete Account inside the app, or by emailing developerlife69@gmail.com. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

Children

Money Control is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

  • Access a copy of your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional permissions (SMS, camera, notifications) at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at developerlife69@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be announced via an in-app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
